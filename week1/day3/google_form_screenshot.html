Application Assessment for Software Development Level 3 Course

Welcome to the Software Development Level 3 course application assessment. This assessment is designed to ensure that you have the necessary skills, technical proficiency, and commitment to succeed in our fully online training program. You will be asked to complete a series of tasks that involve basic computer operations, following instructions, and demonstrating your understanding of English communication.

Each task must be completed independently and submitted via email to admin@lead-ahead.co.uk using the specified subject line for each. The purpose is not to test your knowledge of software development, but to confirm that you have the foundational skills required to engage effectively with an online learning environment and to commit fully to our 16-week program.

Please take your time, read each task carefully, and ensure that your submissions reflect your best effort. Good luck, and we look forward to reviewing your application!
